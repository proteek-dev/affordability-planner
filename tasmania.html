      Tasmania Affordability Checker

      Introduction

      The Tasmania Affordability Checker is a helpful tool for Tasmania families considering their next residential move. The Tasmania Affordability Checker allows users to easily locate potentially suitable areas for the housing move based on their expected average rent information on local schools (including general performance) to help plan for future childhood education needs.

      ** Rental figures are up to date as at June 2023
